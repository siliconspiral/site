Silicon Spiral showcases the Generative Art of Safe Hammad. Generative Art is a term mostly used to mean art wholly or partly generated automatically via pre-determined rules, in my case, by a computer program.

In the past I have tended to create highly geometric pieces, however my current practice, showcased here, is biased to what might be described as more “natural” or “organic” looking pieces.
